Providing high quality, best-practice mental health support to clients and the community.

Q Psychology has clinics in St Kilda, Carlton, and Geelong, and also delivers services nationwide via Telehealth. We support a diverse range of clients and are leaders in LGBTQIAP+ affirming and neuroaffirming mental healthcare.

Reach out to us
Our journey

Q Psychology was founded with a purpose of supporting and improving mental health for all people. From our general psychology services, assessment services, group therapy programs, and research projects, right through to establishing Victoria's first private Gender Service, we have continually sought to do things differently - to go beyond the typical and to be where we are needed.

From small beginnings, Q Psychology has expanded to deliver services in-person across its three clinic locations (St Kilda, Carlton, and Geelong), via its outreach services to regional areas (e.g., Colac), and online throughout Australia via Telehealth.

At Q Psychology, we understand your individual experiences, identity, and needs are unique and diverse. We therefore pride ourselves on delivering a tailored and personalised approach when caring for your mental health.

We are dedicated to creating safe and affirming environments. We believe in respect, inclusivity.

Our core values
Community

We acknowledge and respect the importance of our work with, and connection to, various communities (particularly the LGBTQIAP+ community). We will strive to understand the specific contexts and experiences of the communities we support.

We will work to improve their mental health, and their lives, both at an individual level and at a community level. We will work to empower, and advocate for, disadvantaged communities. Internally, we will always strive to build a community, family, and connected culture within our team.
