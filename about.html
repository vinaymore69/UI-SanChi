Sanchi is a platform dedicated to the education and empowerment of girls. We exist to create opportunities for young girls to access quality education, nurturing their potential and enabling them to build a brighter future. By supporting Sanchi, you help break barriers to education, fostering self-confidence, independence, and a path out of poverty. Our mission is to empower every girl to dream, learn, and achieve, ensuring they have the tools to thrive and succeed in life.

Who   We   Are

We’re the people who believe in second chances. Who believe that we are better together. Who believe in people, until they believe in themselves again.

It all began with a shared vision to uplift and empower young girls through education. Our committee started small, with a handful of passionate individuals determined to address the barriers that prevent girls from accessing quality learning opportunities. By focusing on mentorship, skill development, and community support, our efforts quickly gained momentum, reaching more lives than we ever imagined.

The idea was simple yet powerful: create opportunities for girls to dream big and achieve their potential. We began by organizing workshops, providing scholarships, and collaborating with educators to deliver impactful programs.

Over time, our initiatives expanded to include career counseling, leadership training, and outreach efforts in underprivileged communities. Our greatest impact comes from fostering confidence and self-reliance among the girls we support. By equipping them with the tools and encouragement they need, we help them transform challenges into stepping stones toward success.

At the heart of it all, we are a group of dedicated individuals committed to creating a brighter future. Together, with the support of like-minded partners and the community, we continue to champion the cause of girls' education, one step at a time.

Vision &  Mission

Our Purpose, Vision, and Values guide and shape our approach and decision-making at every level of the committee. They provide the foundation for our efforts, uniting us around a common goal: to empower and uplift young girls through education and opportunities that foster growth, equality, and success.

Vision

To empower every girl with access to quality education, helping her reach her full potential and become confident leaders in a more inclusive and equitable world.

Mission

To provide education, mentorship, and resources that enable girls to thrive academically and personally, creating a supportive environment where they feel valued and capable of achieving their dreams.

Values
